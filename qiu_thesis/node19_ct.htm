RA-PDF experiment

Our first PDF experiment with an IP detector took place in October, 2002. Two essential components were key to the success of this first experiment. One was the experience with the online image plate detector, mainly from Dr. Peter Chupas in Prof. Clare Grey's group at Stony Brook University, Dr. Jon Hanson from Brookhaven National Lab, and Dr. Peter Lee from Argonne National Lab. The other was the expertise with the PDF technique from our group. Part of the results have been published [61]. Technical descriptions, together with the experimental setup diagram, can be found in Appendix A.
