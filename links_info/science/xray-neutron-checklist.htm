Checklist for an x-ray or neutron experiment trip

  • samples
  • sample holders
  • sample mounts (for capillary, for example)
  • kapton tape/saran wrap/aluminum tape
  • tweezers
  • kimwipes
  • DUCO cement
  • superglue
  • overhead pens
  • regular pens
  • notebooks
  • reference books (Klug and Alexander)
  • relevant papers (containing structures etc.)
  • all preliminary data/plots on the samples
  • glue-stick
  • scotch tape
  • scissors
  • 200-gauge sieve
  • base for sieve
  • calipers
  • calculator
  • all forms needed(Passport,Driver's licence,etc..)
  • Money for food and some gifts(T-shirt)
