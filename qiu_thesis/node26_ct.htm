New data analysis program: PDFgetX2

PDFgetX2 is a GUI driven program to obtain the pair distribution function from X-ray powder diffraction data. All codes used now are written by me under the guidance of Prof. Simon Billinge. All source codes amount to more than 40,000 lines.
